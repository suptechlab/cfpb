CFPB Hack Day

Thursday, April 14, 2016
9 AM-4 PM
Room 1101


What is it?

A day of making things, including external resources for the public and internal tools - websites, apps, gadgets, games, and more - to support the CFPB's mission and help government run more efficiently. There will be pizza.

Who's Invited?

All CFPB employees and contractors are invited to attend and propose projects for the event.

How can I participate?

Have a project idea? Propose it here.

Want to contribute your subject matter expertise, or design or code on a project? Join a project here.

Or come see the demos of what everyone made at 3pm in room 1101.

Questions?

Email Catherine.Farman@cfpb.gov or post a question on Github.
