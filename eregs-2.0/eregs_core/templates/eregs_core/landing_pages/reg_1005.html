Regulation E protects consumers when they use electronic fund transfers.

Electronic fund transfers include:

  • ATMs
  • Direct Deposit
  • Gift cards
  • Overdraft
  • Point of sale transfers
  • Remittances
  • Telephone transfers

The regulation covers topics such as:

  • Disclosure of fees and limits
  • Error correction
  • Liability
  • Preauthorized transfers
  • Receipts
