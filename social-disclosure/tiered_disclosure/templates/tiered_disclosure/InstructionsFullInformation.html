In each round, you will be assigned a preference profile and asked to choose one (1) product from a list of products. Each product gives you a certain amount of tokens, determined by the interaction of the product's attributes and your preference profile.

As an example, if your preference profile were the following:

Your Preference Profile
1
2
3

This means that you value attribute A with intensity 1, attribute B with intensity 2, and attribute C with intensity 3.

If you were shown two products, product 1 and product 2, which had different attribute values as shown in the following table:

Product 1 Product 2
5 1
1 1
1 3

Then you would receive 1*5+2*1+3*1=10 tokens for product 1, and 1*1+2*1+3*3=12 tokens for product 2. The best choice for you would be product 2 in this round because you would receive more tokens from product 2 than product 1.

Remember, at the end of the experiment, the tokens you earned in each round will be converted to dollars at a fixed exchange rate.
